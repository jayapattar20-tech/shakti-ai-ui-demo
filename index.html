// How it works

Intelligence that
works in seconds

SHAKTI's AI runs continuously in the background, analyzing your environment — silently protecting you every moment.

01
Continuous background monitoring
The app runs silently in the background, polling mic, accelerometer, and GPS. On-device AI consumes less than 3% battery per hour in standby mode.
02
Multi-signal threat detection
When a potential threat is detected, the AI fuses voice tone, motion pattern, and location risk score into a single confidence value. SOS only triggers above 75% confidence.
03
10-second confirmation window
A countdown gives you 10 seconds to cancel a false alarm. If no action — SOS escalates automatically to all trusted contacts.
04
Parallel emergency actions
GPS streaming, audio/video recording, cloud upload, contact notification, and SMS fallback all fire simultaneously the moment SOS is confirmed.
